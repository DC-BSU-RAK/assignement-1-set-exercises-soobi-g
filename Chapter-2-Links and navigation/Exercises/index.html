Outer Banks

Adventure. Mystery. Treasure Hunt.

Overview

Outer Banks is a Netflix series set in North Carolina city between wealthy people "Kooks" and working-class "Pogues."

The story follows John B and his friends as they embark on a dangerous treasure hunt linked to John B's missing father. Along the way, they uncover secrets, face rival groups, and get caught in thrilling adventures.

Created by Josh Pate, Jonas Pate, and Shannon Burke, the show premiered in April 2020 and quickly became a hit.
